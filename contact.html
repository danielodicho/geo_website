A Note About Trail Recommendations

If you're asking about a specific trail, please include details like your hiking experience level, how far you're willing to drive from Asheville, and what you're hoping to see (waterfalls, views, wildflowers, etc.). This helps me provide the most relevant recommendations!

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer guided hikes?

I don't currently offer guided hikes, but I'm happy to recommend local guides who specialize in the trails featured on this blog.

Can I share your trail guides on social media?

Absolutely! I appreciate when readers share links to my blog posts. If you share photos from trails featured here, I'd love a tag @HiddenTrailsAVL.

How current is your trail information?

I update trail posts whenever I revisit a location, and I note the last update date at the bottom of each guide. Always check for trail closures before heading out.

Do you accept guest posts?

I occasionally feature guest writers who have expertise on specific trails or outdoor topics relevant to the Asheville area. Contact me with your pitch!
